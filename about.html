This app was created to help the casual fantasy football fan make better decisions. This app will allow users to easily make start/sit decisions and feel confident in them.

This app is very simple to use. Just select two players you are having troube deciding between and click compare. The app will do the rest.

The data in this app comes from the Tank01 NFL Live In-Game Real Time Statistics NFL API via rapidapi.com.
